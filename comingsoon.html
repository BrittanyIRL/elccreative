What's next

As always, we have a couple irons in the fire. We still have a few more experiments to work through in the visual novel style of Pills. Mostly, we just love finding ways to bring the point-and-click adventure games of our childhood into unseemly new contexts (like cubicles).

Meanwhile, Brittany is working on a hodgepodge of websites and ISO apps and Gerrit is writing too many books at once. We are probably not caught up on your favorite television show.
