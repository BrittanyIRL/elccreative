Pills

About

One of the hardest life transitions we've come into contact with in our quarter of a century is saying goodbye to the freedoms of student existence and entering the daily ritual of a post-college early career. Every day, you take the same path to the same building, while the rest of the world keeps on changing around you. And as the years go by, enough time to yourself lends to a more anxious perspective.

Pills is our attempt to bottle this feeling. Every day is a blessing, though sometimes it's hard to see this fact clearly through the stress or the monotony. The resulting choose-your-own-adventure mixture is one of humor and melancholy, each in as copious a quantity as you want to see. Pills tries to find simple satisfaction in the remnants of humanity left scattered around the hamster wheel.
